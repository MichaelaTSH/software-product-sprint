Random Skills I've Tried Over The Years

 

Tap Dance

I tried tap dancing for one year after watching Singin' In The Rain. Unfortunately, I was quickly disillusioned when I realised two things. Firstly, it would be years before I could even attempt anything close to what I'd seen in the movie. Secondly, I sucked. It's a fond memory that I look back on and laugh at.

Animation

I joined the Infocomm club in 2009, which was mainly an Animation club with an odd dash of Computer Science. I was taught animation techniques, but was also given the opportunity to make games and websites (without writing any code). I even joined a national competition for animation! Here's the kicker: I made it through all 7 years without being able to draw well. I did improve though!
